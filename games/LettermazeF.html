📜 How to Play:

  • 👉 Move the player 👦 using Arrow Keys, Click, or Swipe.
  • 👉 Follow the correct Target Letter shown at the top bar.
  • 👉 Reach the 🏁 Goal by stepping only on correct target letters.
  • 👉 You can use the 💡 Hint button up to 5 times per level.
  • 👉 Stepping on a wrong letter ❌ sends you back!
